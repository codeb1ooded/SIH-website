PROPOSED SOLUTION

The main attraction of our application (website and app) is that it solves a serious problem in our education system. On one hand where private school children are given quality education, government school students lag behind.

This problem needed attention because educating the youth of a country directly relates to its future. If we want India to progress, we have to educate each and every child .For which we need to remove various barriers in their learning. Non- availability of teachers contributes significantly to it. Hence, it is the need of the hour to look into the issue.

Through our interactive and user friendly application, we have made the process of updating vacancies for teachers and improving quality of education which is imparted to the students, easy and effective.
